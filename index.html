About Us

FOUAD EL WATAN
BKR international

Established in 1989, Fouad El-Watan quickly became known as one of key players in the fields of auditing and tax & financial consultancy in Egypt. With huge investments, both domestic and foreign, we have since completed countless projects in the areas of integration, auditing and financial consulting, financial feasibility studies, as well as tax consultancy. Taking our success internationally, Fouad El-Watan joined BKR International in 2009. From there on, we shared our success with over 300 partners in more than 70 countries around the globe.

Since inception, it has been our goal to become one of 3 largest consulting firms in Egypt and the MENA region.
We help our clients by providing tax advice, whether on local or international taxes. We meticulously help clients adhere to all tax laws at the lowest economic cost.
